Painting Services

Commercial Interior Painting

Our most common project: common areas, tenant repaints, drywall repair, doors and frames, window frames, stairwells, and elevator cabs. We work nights and weekends when necessary so your tenants never miss a day.

Commercial Exterior Painting

Masonry, stucco, EIFS, metal panels, wood siding — whatever substrate your building presents, we have the coating system to protect and refresh it. Thorough surface prep is included in every exterior project.

High-Rise Exteriors

Swing stage certified

Multi-substrate high-rise exteriors using swing stage and aerial platform equipment. We handle masonry, precast, metal curtainwall, EIFS, and window frame coatings on towers and mid-rise buildings across the Denver metro.

Wood Refinishing

Core doors, trim, millwork, and wood accents throughout your facility. We strip, sand, stain, and seal to bring tired wood back to life — or apply a fresh finish that matches your brand standards. No subbing this out.

Write-On Surfaces

Dry-erase coatings, chalkboard finishes, and magnetic paint applied directly to your existing walls. No panels, no frames — just a smooth, functional writing surface anywhere you want it.

Wallcovering

Removal of existing wallcovering, installation of new commercial-grade vinyl, and custom vinyl graphics. Wallcoverings hide blemishes, wipe clean easily, and look great in medical facilities, elevator lobbies, and high-rise common areas.

Flooring & Concrete

Commercial Flooring

Carpet, luxury vinyl tile (LVT), VCT, hardwood, and homogeneous sheet vinyl — removal, supply, and full installation. We coordinate directly with your building schedule to minimize disruption and handle the full scope from demo to finish.

Industrial Surface Prep

Shot blasting, concrete grinding, waterproofing, deck coatings, and removal of existing coatings. Proper prep is the foundation of every long-lasting floor and deck coating — we never skip it.

Stained & Polished Concrete

Stonetec specialty

Concrete repairs, re-leveling, joint sealants, seal coat, and full surface prep. Acid stains, water-based stains, and multi-step diamond polishing to achieve any sheen level. Transforming plain concrete into a premium floor finish.

Stone & Specialty Surfaces

Marble Cleaning & Sealing

Stonetec specialty

Professional cleaning, polishing, and sealing for all natural stone surfaces: marble, granite, limestone, travertine, slate, terrazzo, and engineered quartz. Restores luster, removes etching and staining, and applies penetrating sealers to protect your investment.

Terrazzo Polishing, Repairs & Restoration

Stonetec specialty

Chip and crack repairs, full regrinding and polishing sequences, divider strip replacement, and finish sealing. We restore terrazzo floors to their original depth and brilliance — a fraction of the cost of replacement.

Specialty Finishing Services

Stonetec specialty

Acetone stain applications, polish overlays, and trowel cove base installation. These techniques add texture, depth, and durability to concrete and masonry surfaces — ideal for lobbies, retail floors, and commercial kitchens.

BrickImaging

Certified installer

A certified BrickImaging installer, we apply this proprietary system to transform plain concrete block, poured concrete, or precast panels into a convincing brick or stone pattern — without demolition or new masonry.

Building Protection & Systems

Symterra Bird Defense

Certified installer

A certified Symterra installer, we apply bird deterrent gel systems to ledges, signs, HVAC equipment, and structural elements. Humane, nearly invisible, and highly effective — protecting your building from the costly damage birds cause.

Build-Back & Drywall Repair

Patch, tape, texture, and paint — full drywall repair and installation as part of tenant improvement projects or standalone repairs. We match existing textures precisely: skip trowel, orange peel, knockdown, and smooth.

Lead Paint Remediation

EPA certified

EPA Lead-Safe certified renovation, repair, and painting (RRP) for buildings containing lead-based paint. Full containment, proper disposal, air monitoring, and documentation — keeping your workers, tenants, and project in compliance.

Wall Protection

FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) panels, Lexan clear polycarbonate, and corner guards installed in high-traffic corridors, kitchens, loading docks, and stairwells. The most cost-effective way to protect walls from impact, moisture, and contamination.

Fire Proofing & Specialty Ceiling

Intumescent fire-resistive coatings for exposed structural steel, plus dryfall paint for high-bay ceilings and specialty applications. We handle the coatings your general contractor doesn't want to touch.

Ceiling Tile Replacement

Individual tile replacement or full grid system installation. We match existing tile patterns, handle stained and water-damaged tiles in occupied spaces, and coordinate with facilities teams for minimum disruption.

Maintenance Programs

Maintenance Contracts

Annual touch-up programs, scheduled walkthroughs, multi-year pricing locks, and emergency response coverage. A maintenance contract with Preferred Painting is the single best investment you can make in your building's long-term appearance.
