Thirty years of steady work. Now, one company that does it all.

From Rob Cooney's first commercial painting job in 1992 to the Stonetec acquisition in 2024 — the story of how Preferred Painting became Colorado's most comprehensive commercial surfaces contractor.

1992
Founded

Rob Cooney starts Preferred Painting

Rob Cooney, a Denver native with a background in commercial property management, founded Preferred Painting with a single-minded focus: deliver high-quality commercial painting with the kind of communication and reliability that building owners could actually count on. The company's first clients were property managers — a market relationship that would define Preferred Painting's growth for the next three decades.

2000s
Growth years

Building the Front Range reputation

Through the 2000s, Preferred Painting grew steadily by doing exactly what Rob set out to do: show up on time, charge what was quoted, and leave the site clean. The team expanded its capabilities to include industrial coatings, high-rise exteriors, and specialty finishes. BOMA and IFMA memberships — and deep involvement in both organizations — cemented Preferred Painting as a trusted partner within Denver's commercial real estate community.

2012
Leadership transition

Chris Miller joins as President

Chris Miller, a University of Iowa graduate with a background in Fortune 500 corporate sales, joined Preferred Painting as President — bringing new energy and a strategic vision for growth. Under his leadership, the company expanded its service offerings, deepened client relationships, and developed the systems and team that could handle larger, more complex projects across the entire Front Range.

2020s
Expanded capabilities

New services, same standards

Responding to client demand, Preferred Painting added a range of specialty services that complemented its core painting work: BrickImaging installations, Symterra bird defense systems, lead paint remediation (EPA certified), commercial flooring, and wallcovering. Each new service line followed the same model — get properly certified, hire people who know the work, and do it right the first time.

2024
Acquisition

Preferred Painting acquires Stonetec

In 2024, Preferred Painting acquired Stonetec — a Denver-area specialist in stained and polished concrete, marble and stone restoration, terrazzo repair, and specialty finishing services. The acquisition brought Stonetec's experienced technicians, proprietary techniques, and deep portfolio of specialty surface projects under the Preferred Painting umbrella. Together, the combined company now offers a scope of services no other commercial painting contractor in Colorado can match.

Today
Present

One company. The full building envelope.

Today, Preferred Painting is Colorado's most comprehensive commercial surfaces contractor — handling everything from exterior painting and industrial coatings to polished concrete floors and terrazzo restoration. Thirty-plus years of relationships, 1,500+ completed jobs, and a 98% repeat client rate. The same philosophy Rob started with in 1992, applied to a much bigger canvas.

The Stonetec acquisition

Adding stone, concrete, and specialty surfaces to our toolkit

The Stonetec acquisition wasn't about getting bigger — it was about getting better. Denver's commercial real estate market increasingly wants a single partner who can handle the full building: paint, floors, stone, concrete, and specialty finishes. Now we are that partner.

Stonetec's team brings decades of experience with surfaces that most painting contractors won't touch: polished concrete, terrazzo restoration, marble and granite sealing, and proprietary specialty finishes. Their certifications, equipment, and techniques are now available on every Preferred Painting project.

Stained & Polished Concrete

Repairs, re-leveling, joint sealants, seal coat, and multi-step diamond polishing for any sheen level.

Marble, Granite & Stone

Cleaning, polishing, and penetrating sealer applications for all natural stone surfaces.

Terrazzo Restoration

Chip and crack repairs, full regrinding and polishing sequences, and finish sealing.

Specialty Finishing

Acetone stain applications, polish overlays, and trowel cove base — for lobbies, retail, and kitchens.
