Why Preferred Painting for senior living

Quiet crews. Low-odor products. Zero disruption.

Senior residents are home around the clock. Our crews are trained to work quietly, respectfully, and efficiently — in occupied spaces where disruption is not an option.

Low-odor & zero-VOC coatings

We use low-odor, zero-VOC interior paints appropriate for occupied senior spaces — protecting respiratory health and keeping residents comfortable throughout the process.

Respectful crew conduct

Our painters are trained to work in environments where residents may be vulnerable. Quiet, professional, and courteous — or we don't send them back.

Off-hours options available

For high-activity areas like dining rooms and activity lounges, we can schedule overnight or early-morning work — having the space restored and odor-free before residents arrive for breakfast.

Wall protection systems

Wheelchairs, walkers, and mobility equipment take a toll on walls. FRP panels, Lexan, and corner guards protect corridors and resident room entrances from daily impact damage.

Communication every step

Daily updates to your facility director. Clear advance notice before any work begins in a residential wing. We keep your staff informed so they can keep residents comfortable.

Maintenance programs available

Annual touch-up schedules keep your community looking its best year-round — without the disruption and cost of a full repaint every few years.
