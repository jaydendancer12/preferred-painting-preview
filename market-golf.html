Why Preferred Painting for golf courses

Seasonal expertise. UV-rated systems. Member-worthy finishes.

Golf course facilities face unique challenges: extreme UV, freeze-thaw cycles, and members who notice everything. We address all three with the right systems and the right scheduling.

Off-season & winter scheduling

We plan and mobilize when the season ends, guaranteeing completion before reopening — not after your first member arrives.

UV-resistant exterior coatings

Colorado's altitude means significantly higher UV exposure than sea level. We specify elastomeric and UV-stable systems that resist fading, chalking, and premature failure.

Member-grade interior finishes

Club members expect a premium environment. Dining rooms, locker rooms, bar areas, and pro shops — finished to a level that meets your membership's expectations.

Wood refinishing expertise

Clubhouses are full of wood — beams, paneling, trim, locker room doors, and bar millwork. We strip, sand, stain, and seal to restore original character without costly replacement.

Maintenance facility coatings

Maintenance buildings and cart storage require industrial-grade floor and wall coatings that handle equipment, oils, and cleaning chemicals — scoped separately from the clubhouse.

Fixed-price quotes

Golf club capital budgets are set months in advance. Our fixed-price approach gives your board and finance committee the certainty they need to approve the project.
