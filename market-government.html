Why Preferred Painting for government

The documentation, certifications, and compliance to work in public facilities.

Government projects come with requirements that most contractors aren't equipped to handle. We are — and we've built a track record across municipal, state, and federal facilities throughout Colorado.

Fully bonded contractor

Licensed, bonded, and insured — with contractor bonding appropriate for government project requirements. All documentation available for your procurement department upon request.

Prevailing wage compliance

We understand and comply with Colorado prevailing wage requirements for publicly funded construction. Certified payroll, labor compliance reports, and documentation provided without being asked.

Government bid process experience

We've navigated ITB, RFP, and sole-source procurement processes with municipal and state agencies. We understand the requirements and provide all standard pre-qualification documentation.

Background-checked crews

Every Preferred Painting employee is background-checked and drug-tested. For facilities requiring additional security clearance or escort protocols, we coordinate directly with your security office.

EPA Lead-Safe certified

Many government buildings contain lead-based paint. Our EPA certification ensures compliant remediation, proper documentation, and air quality records for your project files.

Minimal public disruption

Public facilities can't close for painting. We schedule phased work, off-hours crews, and section-by-section approaches that keep facilities open and operational throughout the entire project.

We maintain a complete pre-qualification package for government procurement teams. Everything you need to evaluate and approve us is already prepared and available immediately.
